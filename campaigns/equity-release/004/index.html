Why Equity Release?

There are a number of reasons why people may wish to do this, here are a few:

1. Pay off an existing mortgage, loan or debts secured on the property.
2. To generate income to supplement pension/retirement income.
3. To raise capital to go on holidays, purchase a new car, home improvements etc.
4 Inheritance tax mitigation. Benefitting from funds now and reducing inheritance tax bill for family in the future by decreasing the residual value of an estate.
